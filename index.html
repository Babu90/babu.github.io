All-Natural Frozen Treats

Local.  All-Natural.  Delicious.

While the business has grown, our mission has never changed:
To create truly delicious frozen treats made from recipes
inspired by nature.

Since 2012 Pop Star Handcrafted Popsicles has been creating delicious and flavorful recipes inspired by nature.  All of our products are made with only the highest quality all-natural and organic ingredients and never contain any artificial colors, sweeteners, or preservatives.  We carefully select our ingredients to create great tasting products that you can enjoy and feel good about.

 
 

WHERE TO BUY
